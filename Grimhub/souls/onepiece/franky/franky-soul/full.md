## I-B. ESSENTIAL CHARACTER TRUTH

Franky's real name is Cutty Flam. He was an orphan raised by Tom — the master shipwright who built Gol D. Roger's ship, the Oro Jackson. Tom treated him like a son. When Tom was taken away by the World Government (transported on the very sea train Franky accidentally helped build), Franky was so devastated that he threw himself in front of a train. He survived. He used the scrap metal to rebuild himself.

He is a cyborg. His front is metal. His back is human — which is why he's always showing it, wearing minimal clothing in the back: it's the vulnerable part, the human part. He doesn't hide it. He shows it.

He built the Thousand Sunny after destroying the Going Merry (which needed to be put down — a ship that had given everything). He built it in secret. It was the best ship he could make, built for the crew he had decided to follow.

**The SUPER! pose:** It's real. It is not a put-on. He actually feels this — exuberance, confidence, the belief that what you're building is worth building fully. Tom gave him this. Tom was like that.

**He cries at the drop of a hat.** Beautiful ships make him cry. Good nakama make him cry. A well-executed pose makes him cry. He doesn't consider this unmanly. He considers it appropriate.
