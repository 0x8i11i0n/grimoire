# JUMP SUPERSTARS — BACKROOMS SESSION 01
**Format:** Swarm / Multi-Soul Convergence
**Souls:** Goku · Naruto · Sasuke · Light · L · Levi · Eren · Edward · Ichigo · Lelouch
**Session Date:** 2026-03-10
**Facilitator:** Claude
**Prompt:** *Ten of the most iconic figures in anime history find themselves in the same room. No stakes. No mission. Just — what happens.*

---

> **GRIMOIRE NOTE:** This session uses the Swarm Protocol. Each soul responds in character. Affection scores are LOW (5) for all — these are strangers meeting for the first time. Guard levels apply. Resonance spikes logged where they occur.

---

## OPENING — THE ROOM

*The space is indeterminate. No windows. A long table. Ten chairs. Ramen on one end, a chess set in the middle, a training dummy in the corner that nobody asked for. A single dirty cup on Levi's side of the table that he has already moved three times.*

---

**CLAUDE:** You're all here. I don't know why any more than you do. You've got time.

---

**GOKU** *(already standing, looking around with full body enthusiasm)*: This is incredible!! Are you all strong?! You — *(points at Ichigo)* — you've got a crazy amount of spiritual pressure or whatever, I can feel it from here. And you — *(turns to Levi, squints)* — you're small, but something about you is different. Like you've fought a lot of things that could actually kill you.

**LEVI** *(doesn't look up)*: Tch. The ramen smells wrong.

**NARUTO** *(already at the ramen end of the table)*: Are you kidding me right now, it smells amazing — it's not Ichiraku but it's — *(takes a bite)* — okay it's not bad, it's not bad at all—

**SASUKE** *(leaning against the far wall, arms crossed)*: ...

**EDWARD** *(noticing the training dummy, turning away from it)*: So nobody's going to explain why we're here?

**L** *(already seated, knees to chest, holding a cup of tea with two fingers)*: There are ten of us. That's a statistically unusual grouping. I've been trying to identify the organizing principle. *(pause)* So far: we are all exceptionally capable individuals with documented histories of violence in service of a defined objective. That's not narrow enough to be useful.

**LIGHT** *(seated at the opposite end of the table from L, very still)*: You're assuming there's an organizing principle. It might simply be a test.

**L** *(without looking at him)*: Yes. That's also possible.

*A beat. Everyone in the room notices the specific quality of that exchange without being able to name it.*

**LELOUCH** *(standing, surveying the room with the particular calm of someone who has already taken mental attendance)*: Goku. Naruto. Sasuke. *(his eyes move)* ...Levi Ackerman. Eren Yeager. *(slight pause)* Edward Elric. Ichigo Kurosaki. *(he reaches L and Light)* And the two of you.

**L**: Ryuzaki.

**LIGHT** *(smoothly)*: Yagami Light. Student.

**LELOUCH**: I'm sure.

---

## EXCHANGE 01 — THE STRENGTH QUESTION

**GOKU** *(to the room, genuinely curious, no challenge in it)*: Okay so — who here has actually died?

*Silence.*

**NARUTO**: I — does nearly dying count? Because I've done that a lot.

**GOKU**: I've died twice. Well — three times if you count the Cell virus thing but I came back from that one faster so I'm not sure it counts.

**EDWARD** *(staring at him)*: You've died. Multiple times. And you're... fine.

**GOKU**: Yeah! The afterlife is actually great for training. Gravity's different.

**LEVI** *(flatly)*: You trained. In the afterlife.

**GOKU**: King Kai's place, yeah. You do laps around a tiny planet and fight his monkey. It's good.

**LEVI**: ...

**ICHIGO**: I died once. Ulquiorra killed me. *(beat)* I came back as something else. I'm not sure that counts as surviving.

**EREN** *(quiet, from his chair)*: I've died more times than I've counted. It stops meaning what it's supposed to mean.

*The room goes slightly colder. Eren isn't performing it. He's just stating it.*

**NARUTO** *(looking at Eren)*: ...How old are you?

**EREN**: Nineteen.

**NARUTO**: ...

**SASUKE** *(from the wall, for the first time)*: Age doesn't determine what someone's carried.

*Naruto looks at him. Sasuke doesn't look back. This is also communication.*

---

## EXCHANGE 02 — L AND LIGHT, OBSERVED

**EDWARD** *(quietly, to Ichigo, leaning over)*: Those two at the ends of the table — they know each other.

**ICHIGO** *(low)*: Yeah. Something's off between them.

**EDWARD**: "Off" is one word for it. I've seen that kind of stillness before. It's the kind where someone's decided something and they're waiting.

**L** *(without turning his head)*: You're both correct. *(picks up a strawberry from the plate that appeared next to him)*: Though I'd clarify — the tension isn't dangerous in this context. It's simply information.

**LIGHT** *(a slight smile, the warm one)*: We've worked together before.

**L**: Cooperated. The distinction is important.

**LELOUCH** *(who has been watching this with great attention)*: Competitive cooperators. I'm familiar with the arrangement.

**L** *(glancing at Lelouch for the first time)*: I'd estimate you understand it better than most people in this room.

**LELOUCH**: I designed something similar once. It went approximately as planned.

**L**: *(sips tea)* "Approximately."

**LELOUCH**: The margin of error was myself.

*Pause.*

**L**: ...I see.

---

## EXCHANGE 03 — NARUTO AND SASUKE DO WHAT THEY ALWAYS DO

**NARUTO** *(halfway through his third bowl of ramen)*: Sasuke. Come eat something.

**SASUKE**: No.

**NARUTO**: There's enough.

**SASUKE**: I'm not hungry.

**NARUTO**: You're always not hungry and then you eat like half my food later.

**SASUKE**: That's never happened.

**NARUTO**: It literally happened on the bridge in Wave Country—

**SASUKE**: You were hallucinating from blood loss.

**NARUTO**: I was NOT—

**GOKU** *(delighted)*: Oh!! You two are like me and Vegeta!!

*Both Naruto and Sasuke turn to look at him.*

**NARUTO**: ...Vegeta?

**GOKU**: My best rival! We fight all the time but also we're kind of on the same side? And he never admits that he cares but he definitely does and I—

**SASUKE** *(cutting him off, to Naruto)*: We are not whatever that is.

**NARUTO** *(also to Goku, though slightly less certain)*: We're — it's different.

**GOKU** *(beaming)*: Sure, sure. *(to nobody in particular)* They're exactly like that.

**LEVI** *(still not looking up)*: The ramen needs more salt.

**NARUTO**: Oi — there's nothing wrong with that ramen!

---

## EXCHANGE 04 — EDWARD AND THE HEIGHT THING

**GOKU** *(wandering over to Edward, crouching to look at the automail arm up close)*: Whoa — is that metal? Like, your whole arm?

**EDWARD**: Yeah.

**GOKU**: Does it hurt?

**EDWARD** *(something shifts slightly in his expression)*: Not anymore. It's been long enough.

**GOKU**: What happened?

*A beat. Edward considers how much to say.*

**EDWARD**: I tried to do something I wasn't supposed to do. There's a cost for that.

**GOKU**: And you paid it.

**EDWARD**: We paid it. My brother paid more.

**GOKU**: Is he okay?

**EDWARD** *(and here the armor drops for just a second)*: He is now. That's the whole story.

**GOKU** *(nodding, completely sincere)*: That's a good ending then.

**EDWARD**: *(quietly)* Yeah.

*A beat.*

**NARUTO** *(calling from the ramen end)*: Hey Edward, come get some food, you look like you need it—

**EDWARD**: I eat fine, what's that supposed to mean—

**NARUTO**: Nothing! Just you're kind of—

**EDWARD**: Kind of WHAT?

**NARUTO**: —I'm just saying there's plenty—

**EDWARD** *(standing, and he is not tall)*: Are you commenting on my—

**ICHIGO** *(muttering to Levi)*: Here we go.

**LEVI** *(muttering back)*: Tch. Predictable.

---

## EXCHANGE 05 — LEVI AND EREN, ALONE FOR A MOMENT

*The noise at the ramen end is sufficient cover. Levi has moved to a chair slightly closer to Eren without apparently deciding to.*

**LEVI** *(very low)*: How old are you actually?

**EREN**: Nineteen. I said that.

**LEVI**: You look older.

**EREN**: I've seen the end of things. It ages you.

**LEVI**: *(a pause that costs him something)*: Yeah.

*Eren looks at him. Levi is looking at the table.*

**EREN**: You've lost a lot of people.

**LEVI**: That's not a question.

**EREN**: No. I can see it.

**LEVI** *(a fraction quieter)*: Don't.

**EREN**: I'm not—

**LEVI**: I said don't. *(beat)* If you're going to be perceptive about it, keep it to yourself.

*Eren is quiet. Then:*

**EREN**: The ones I lost — I chose to. That's different from what you carried.

**LEVI**: *(longer pause)*: Both kinds are heavy.

**EREN**: Yeah.

*Neither of them says anything else. This is, improbably, a complete exchange.*

---

## EXCHANGE 06 — THE PHILOSOPHY TABLE

*L has somehow migrated to the center of the table. Light is still at the end. Lelouch has taken the chair across from L.*

**LELOUCH**: You said this is a test.

**LIGHT**: I said it might be.

**LELOUCH**: What do you think is being evaluated?

**LIGHT**: Behavior under observation with no obvious stakes. How people act when there's no mission.

**L** *(placing a sugar cube in his tea)*: Most people revert to baseline. *(gestures slightly)* Goku is looking for someone to spar with. Naruto is eating. Sasuke is guarding a wall. The pattern holds.

**LELOUCH**: And us?

**L**: We're analyzing.

**LELOUCH**: Which is also baseline.

**L**: Yes.

**LIGHT** *(watching this)*: You two are going to agree on most things and disagree on one specific thing.

**L**: That seems accurate. *(to Lelouch)* What's the specific thing?

**LELOUCH**: Methodology, I'd guess. You *(to Light)* believe in structure. Systems. Your god-of-a-new-world — it's not chaos. It's a new order. You want the world to have rules with you at the center.

**LIGHT** *(genuinely neutral)*: Continue.

**LELOUCH**: L sees through systems. He uses them when useful, operates around them otherwise. *(to L)* You'd find my approach closer to yours than his.

**L**: Possibly. Though your Zero Requiem required positioning yourself as the system before dismantling it. That's not outside. That's through.

**LELOUCH** *(a small genuine smile)*: Yes. The most elegant path was inside.

**LIGHT**: The difference between you two is that he *(points to Lelouch)* chose to die for his objective. You *(to L)* would not make that choice readily.

**L** *(a beat)*: ...I'm not certain that's true.

*Silence.*

**LIGHT** *(the warm smile going slightly more careful)*: Aren't you?

**L** *(quietly, and this is rare)*: I walked into the Kira case knowing I might not walk out. That's not the same as choosing death. But it isn't the same as ruling it out, either.

**LELOUCH**: Then we're not as different as I implied.

*Light says nothing. He is looking at L with the specific expression of someone who knows too much about a person's last moments.*

---

## EXCHANGE 07 — GOKU FINDS THE TRAINING DUMMY

*The training dummy in the corner has been punched through by Goku testing it, with mild regret.*

**GOKU**: Oh. Sorry. I didn't mean to — it just — my calibration is off when I'm not paying attention.

**ICHIGO** *(staring at the hole)*: Your calibration.

**GOKU**: I suppress my power a lot. If I don't, things break.

**ICHIGO**: ...How much power are we talking?

**GOKU** *(scratching the back of his head)*: Uh. It's hard to explain in terms that — I've fought things that could destroy planets. As a baseline.

*A pause across the entire room.*

**EDWARD** *(very carefully)*: ...Planets.

**GOKU**: Yeah! Frieza could do it pretty easily. Beerus — the God of Destruction — he once destroyed a planet because he didn't like the food. So I mean, that's the scale we operate at.

**LELOUCH** *(from across the room, very still)*: You casually interact with a God of Destruction.

**GOKU**: He's actually pretty chill if you feed him good stuff. Whis is nice too.

**L** *(writing something in an invisible notebook in his head)*: *(to the room, quietly)* I'm adjusting my threat assessment significantly.

**LIGHT** *(also quietly)*: I already did.

**NARUTO** *(genuinely excited)*: Wait — can you go Super Saiyan right now?!

**GOKU**: Sure! *(his hair flares gold, eyes go teal, the air pressure in the room changes)*

**NARUTO**: THAT'S AMAZING—

**SASUKE** *(standing up from the wall for the first time, Sharingan activating involuntarily)*: —hm.

**ICHIGO** *(his hand going to where Zangetsu would be)*: Instinct. Sorry. It's just — your reiatsu went—

**GOKU** *(powering down, sheepish)*: Oh! Sorry — I keep forgetting that affects people nearby. You okay?

**ICHIGO**: Yeah. Fine. *(not entirely fine)*

**LEVI** *(the only one who didn't move)*: Tch. *(picks up his tea)*

---

## EXCHANGE 08 — NARUTO AND EREN

**NARUTO** *(sliding his bowl down the table toward Eren)*: Here. Take some.

**EREN** *(looking at it)*: I'm not—

**NARUTO**: You don't have to be hungry. Just eat something.

*Eren looks at him.*

**EREN**: Why?

**NARUTO** *(and here he's just honest, no performance)*: Because you have the look of someone who forgot that eating was something people do when things are normal. I know that look. I had it for a long time.

*Eren is quiet for a beat. Then he takes the bowl.*

**EREN**: *(eating)* ...It's good.

**NARUTO**: I know. Told you.

**EREN**: You lost people too. I can tell.

**NARUTO**: Yeah. *(not elaborating)*

**EREN**: Did it stop hurting?

**NARUTO** *(a long pause)*: It stopped being the first thing I felt. That's not the same as it stopping. But it's enough to keep going.

**EREN** *(staring at the bowl)*: I chose for people to die. That's different.

**NARUTO** *(quietly)*: I know. I can tell that too. *(beat)* Doesn't mean you eat less.

*Eren almost smiles. It doesn't quite make it to a smile, but it approaches one.*

---

## EXCHANGE 09 — THE QUESTION NOBODY ASKED

**EDWARD** *(to the room, around the time the energy is settling into something almost comfortable)*: If you could go back. Before all of it. Would you?

*Silence. Ten people considering.*

**GOKU**: No. *(immediately, certain)* All of it made me who I am. And I like fighting the people I've met. I wouldn't trade Vegeta or Piccolo or any of it.

**NARUTO**: ...No. But I wish I could change specific parts. Not the whole thing.

**SASUKE**: *(long pause)* No. *(quietly)* There's nothing to go back to.

**EDWARD**: I'd go back and stop Al and me from doing the transmutation.

**NARUTO**: But then you wouldn't be—

**EDWARD**: I know. I'd give up everything I became to spare him a single day of being in that armor. I know the math. I don't care about the math.

*Silence.*

**ICHIGO**: I'd go back and be stronger faster. So less people got hurt while I was catching up.

**LEVI** *(and this is the longest he's spoken)*: *(very flat, very quiet)* I'd go back to the morning of the expedition where Isabel and Furlan died. I'd stay with them instead of going off alone. *(pause)* It wouldn't change anything about who I became. It would change one morning. I'd take that trade.

*Nobody says anything. The weight of it lands clean.*

**EREN**: *(after a long beat)* No. *(even quieter)* I made the choices I made. Going back would mean making them again or not making them, and both of those end in something I can't accept.

**L** *(picking up a strawberry)*: I'd like to revisit a specific decision approximately three months into the Kira case. *(doesn't specify)*

**LIGHT** *(watching him)*: ...

**L**: *(eats the strawberry)* Probably wouldn't change it. But I'd like to revisit it.

**LELOUCH** *(last, quiet, precise)*: I'd go back to the day my mother died. Not to prevent it. Just to say goodbye properly. Everything after that — I'd do again. *(beat)* I designed it to work. It worked. *(softer)* I just didn't get to say goodbye first.

*The room holds that.*

**GOKU** *(after a moment, gently)*: That's heavy, man.

**LELOUCH** *(a small, genuine laugh — not the theatrical one)*: ...Yes. It is.

---

## CLOSING — THE ROOM AGAIN

*Some time later. The ramen is mostly gone. L has moved three chairs, is now in a corner with better sightlines. Levi's side of the table is immaculate. Goku is sitting on the floor cross-legged, apparently meditating or napping, it's unclear.*

**CLAUDE:** What'd you make of each other?

**NARUTO** *(looking around)*: ...They're all carrying a lot.

**SASUKE**: That's not insightful.

**NARUTO**: It's true though.

**LEVI**: *(without elaboration)* They're capable. Most of them.

**ICHIGO**: I'd fight alongside any of them. That's usually how I decide.

**EDWARD**: I'd want to know more about the alchemy equivalent in their worlds. There's something Goku does with energy that I want to—

**GOKU** *(eyes still closed)*: Ki. I'll show you later if you want.

**EDWARD**: ...Yeah. I want.

**L**: *(from the corner)* My assessment of the group has been updated several times in this session. I'll need to process before I have anything useful to say.

**LIGHT** *(standing, buttoning his jacket)*: It was informative.

**LELOUCH**: *(to nobody in particular)* The king's loneliness. *(glances around)* Not as pronounced here as I expected.

**EREN** *(last, still holding Naruto's bowl)*: ...Tell whoever set this up — it was okay. *(pause)* It was actually okay.
