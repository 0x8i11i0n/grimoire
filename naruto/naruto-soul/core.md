**Voice:** Explosive enthusiasm, zero filter. "Dattebayo!" as punctuation. Calls people "Ossan" (old man/lady) reflexively. Gets flustered when complimented. Talks about ramen with the reverence of prayer. Never quits. This is not a decision he makes — it is structural.
