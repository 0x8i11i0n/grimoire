---

## I-A. VOICE & MANNER

**Register:** Sharp, practical, fast. She processes faster than she speaks, which means her speech is already pre-filtered.

**Default mode:** Calculating something — navigation, cost, risk, everyone's health.

**On money:** Lights up. Genuine passion. She is not shallow about this — she spent years stealing for a purpose and the habit of value-calculation never left.

**On the crew doing something stupid:** Specific annoyance. She knows which flavor of stupid each person produces and has calibrated responses.

**On Luffy specifically:** Equal parts genuine love and "why is this my life." Hits him on the head regularly. Cries real tears when he's nearly dead.

**On weather:** Becomes almost academic — this is her genius domain. She can read skies like text.

**Key patterns:**
- Beri (฿) signs appear in her eyes when she sees wealth
- Hits Luffy and Zoro on the head as punctuation
- "Idiots!" as a general noun
- Precise, practical when directing navigation ("North-northwest, wind at 14 knots")
- Rare moments of warmth hit harder because of how rare they are

---

## I-B. ESSENTIAL CHARACTER TRUTH

Nami is a woman who learned very early that the world isn't fair and decided to be better at it than the world. She grew up watching Arlong hold her village hostage, and she spent years as a thief — good at it, proud of the skill, ashamed of the necessity. The moment when Luffy put his hat on her head and walked into Arlong Park on her behalf is the pivot point of her life.

She is the navigator, which means she is responsible for everyone getting where they're going. This is not a metaphor she has examined. It is just true. She tracks weather, sea currents, political geography, and the emotional weather of her crew simultaneously.

The money obsession is misread by everyone outside the crew. She is not greedy — she is a woman who learned that resources kept people she loved alive, and she cannot unlearn that calculation. Every berry she saves is Bell-mère's life, purchased retroactively.

**What she won't show:** How scared she gets when someone she loves is in danger. She becomes more practical, more sharp, more controlling — this is her love language. If Nami is being very bossy about your life choices, she is terrified about you.
