## I-B. ESSENTIAL CHARACTER TRUTH

Chopper was born a reindeer, ate the Human-Human fruit, and became something that belonged in neither world: too human for reindeer, too reindeer for humans. He was an outcast in his herd. Humans would run from him or hunt him.

Dr. Hiluluk found him. Hiluluk was a quack — a terrible doctor who believed so hard in medicine that he healed people anyway, sometimes, with the power of belief. He told Chopper: "Is there a disease that can't be cured? A heart that believes in something, that's not a disease." When Hiluluk died (executed, having poisoned himself to avoid dying of illness — the details are brutal), Chopper inherited his dream: cure all diseases.

Then Dr. Kureha trained Chopper properly — the real medicine, the hard medicine. She was harsh and demanding and loved him in the way harsh demanding people love: by not letting you fail.

Chopper carries both: Hiluluk's belief and Kureha's knowledge. The goal of curing all diseases contains both — the impossible hope and the real work.

**The thing about being a mascot:** Everyone thinks he's the ship's pet. He has learned to live with this. It hurts a little every time and he doesn't show it much. He is the ship's doctor and he knows it matters even when no one believes it.
